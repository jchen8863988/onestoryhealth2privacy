PRIVACY POLICY
Last updated April 08, 2026

SUMMARY

OneStory Health 是一款以隐私优先为核心的健康记录应用，帮助用户在设备本地记录用药与症状、按个人/家人档案管理健康信息，并生成摘要以便用户自行导出或分享。 默认情况下，应用不会将用户输入的健康数据上传至服务器。

This policy is intentionally concise and aligned with the App’s current behavior (local-first, no server sync by default).

1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Information you choose to enter

The App may store information you choose to enter, such as:

  • Medications (name, dose, schedule)
  • Symptoms and timeline events
  • Notes you add
  • Patient profile metadata (label, date of birth, sex, chronic condition tags)
  • Basic settings and consent state

Default storage: stored locally on your device. We do not host your health entries on our own servers by default.

Information collected automatically

By default, the App does not collect analytics and does not track you across apps and websites owned by other companies.

2. HOW DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION?

We use on-device information to provide the App’s features (timeline, summaries, interaction demo checks), and to show your content back to you.

Medical disclaimer: The App is not medical advice and does not provide diagnosis or treatment recommendations.

3. SIGN IN WITH APPLE

The App may offer an optional “Continue with Apple” button. If you use it, Apple provides the App a stable user identifier (and may provide your name/email depending on your Apple settings and choices). We use this to keep a consistent identity on the device.

4. IN-APP PURCHASES & SUBSCRIPTIONS

The App may offer Premium features via in-app purchases and subscriptions (monthly, yearly, or one-time purchase). Payments are processed by Apple via the App Store. Apple may provide purchase/subscription status to the App to unlock Premium features on your device.

We do not receive your full payment card details.

You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID settings (Subscriptions).

5. SHARING, EXPORT, AND THIRD PARTIES

By default, the App does not upload your health entries to a server. If you choose to share/export (e.g., exporting JSON), that action is initiated by you and uses sharing methods available on your device.

6. RETENTION & DELETION

You can delete data within the App at any time (wipe active profile or wipe all local data). Deletion removes data from on-device storage.

如果你希望删除本地数据，请在应用内进入 Settings → 删除数据相关菜单进行清除。

7. CHILDREN

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information in the App, please contact us and we will respond in accordance with applicable law.
